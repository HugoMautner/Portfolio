Nydiod

While unfortunately this game never turned out to be finished according to our groups initial idea, it was another great learning experience for me, since I got to work a lot with music scoring and creating sound effects for the game's needs.

The game

Nydiod is a 3d open world third person shooter, where you have to use weapons such as your danish gun, and grenades to survive hoards of creatures and scary turrets.
